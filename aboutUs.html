About Jhutty Transport LTD

Our Focus:
JHUTTY TRANSPORT LTD offers comprehensive, fast, and reliable solutions to meet customer transportation and distribution requirements. Our primary focus is delivering reliable, efficient, and secure freight transportation services across North America.
We provide both team and single-driver services based on customer needs. Our drivers understand the importance of on-time deliveries, and we ensure 24/7 dispatch support to address any unforeseen circumstances.

Our Commitment to Efficiency & Effectiveness:
At JTL, we emphasize two key principles in our business—Efficiency and Effectiveness. Our goal is to provide customers with the best service at the best price, ensuring maximum satisfaction and seamless logistics solutions. We leverage satellite tracking for all our trucks and trailers, enabling real-time updates for customers about their shipments in transit.

Customer-Centric Approach:
We are a growing trucking company, which allows us to maintain a level of customer service flexibility that larger companies don't enjoy. With years of industry experience, we understand the importance of efficiency, safety, and customer satisfaction.

Our team consists of dedicated and experienced transportation professionals who are committed to helping your business succeed. By focusing on the details and maintaining high service standards, we ensure that our trucking services start and end with exceptional customer service.

At JTL, we don’t just transport goods—we partner with you for success!

    Why Choose Us?
  • 24/7 Dispatch Support - Immediate assistance for any transport needs.
  • Real-time GPS Tracking - Know where your shipment is at all times.
  • Reliable & Professional Drivers – Experienced professionals ensuring safe and timely deliveries.
  • Flexible Service Options – Single or team drivers to meet customer timelines.
  • Competitive Pricing – Quality service at the best possible rates.
